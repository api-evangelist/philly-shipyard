# Philly Shipyard (philly-shipyard)
Philly Shipyard (now Hanwha Philly Shipyard, Inc.) is a commercial shipbuilder based at the former Philadelphia Naval Shipyard, constructing Jones Act-compliant container ships, product and Aframax tankers, and National Security Multi-Mission Vessels (NSMVs) for the U.S. Maritime Administration and state maritime academies. In December 2024, Hanwha Systems and Hanwha Ocean (subsidiaries of South Korea's Hanwha Group) completed the acquisition of Philly Shipyard for approximately $100M, rebranding the operation as Hanwha Philly Shipyard Inc. (HPSI). No public developer APIs have been identified at this time.

## Ownership

In December 2024, Hanwha Systems and Hanwha Ocean (subsidiaries of South Korea's Hanwha Group) completed the acquisition of Philly Shipyard for approximately **$100 million**, announced June 20, 2024. The yard now operates as **Hanwha Philly Shipyard, Inc. (HPSI)** at [hanwhaphillyshipyard.com](https://hanwhaphillyshipyard.com/). The legacy `phillyshipyard.com` domain remains live.

> "Philly Shipyard is now a part of the Hanwha family, one of South Korea's largest and most respected business groups." — Hanwha Philly Shipyard

## Product Lines

- Jones Act-compliant container ships (Aloha-class, CV 2600-class, 3,600 TEU)
- Product tankers (50,000 dwt) and Aframax tankers (~115,000 dwt)
- National Security Multi-Mission Vessels (NSMVs) for U.S. state maritime academies
- LNG-fueled container ships
- Maintenance, Repair, and Overhaul (MRO) services for commercial and government customers
